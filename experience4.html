4. Time Dialation

Looking back outside, you see everything move incredibly fast. The rest of space is advancing and changing over time, but you are still the same, only moments have passed. You are watching the universe and your surroundings unfold millions of years into the future. Anyone looking at you from the outside however, will eternally see you at the accretion disk, because the light capturing you has disappeared. Do you persist?
